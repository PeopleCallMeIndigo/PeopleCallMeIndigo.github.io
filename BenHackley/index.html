About me

Delivering the Promise

I am passionate about the creative, entrepreneurial spirit of the small business owner and strive to support and lead them in achieving their financial goals. I simplify and streamline the process with a forward looking focus on growing cash flow and being a valuable partner in the strategic and operational decision process.

I have 30+ years of experience in CFO and financial leadership roles across manufacturing, distribution, service, eCommerce, B2B and D2C industries, encompassing international, Fortune 500, private equity and privately held family businesses. Over my years, I have a strong track record of being the financial voice of reason when advising on growth initiatives, forecasting, cash mangement and internal controls. With a profesional and relaxed manner, this has resulted in productive relationships with management teams, co-workers, external firms, lending institutions, and Boards of Directors. I am a CPA with an accounting degree from James Madison University and MBA from Wake Forest University.

I find great personal reward by putting this vast experience to work for the benefit of small businesses.

On a personal note, when not engrossed in your numbers, I love sports and the outdoors including hiking, backpacking, nature, and traveling with my wife, Laura. being one of 6 children raised by parents of the Great Depression, and a parent myself, within me are instilled the values of work, patience, adaptability, frugality, and flexibility.

What is your availability for new clients?

My priority is providing excellent service and being available to my clients. Therefore, I keep my number of clients in line with my ability to always meet those two goals.

What is your process for taking a new client?

It starts with a phone call to discuss your current state of affairs, pain points and goals, and to determine if we will be a good fit for each other. This evolves to my initial analisis of your business, towards a long term working arrangement.

What is your cost?

This varies by client depending on the complexity of your business and the level of support needed. I do charge an upfront fee for the initial analysis of your business, then charge a fixed monthly fee based on the ongoing scope of work. I do not charge by the hour as it is my expertise, not my time, that I provide.

Do you only consider clients from a certain industry?

I am open to all business because I personally love the variety, and one of my key skills is the ability to sucessfully apply financial concepts across industries.

What type of business have you helped?

Manufacturing, distribution, E-Commerce, Training & Entertainment, Bio Tech, Project Based business, Real Estate Management, etc.
