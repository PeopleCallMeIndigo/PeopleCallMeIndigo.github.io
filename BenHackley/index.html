What is your availability for new clients?

My priority is providing excellent service and being available to my clients. Therefore, I keep my number of clients in line with my ability to always meet those two goals.

What is your process for taking a new client?

It starts with a phone call to discuss your current state of affairs, pain points and goals, and to determine if we will be a good fit for each other. This evolves to my initial análisis of your business, towards a long term working arrangement.

What is your cost?

This varies by client depending on the complexity of your business and the level of support needed. I do charge an upfront fee for the initial analysis of your business, then charge a fixed monthly fee based on the ongoing scope of work.
