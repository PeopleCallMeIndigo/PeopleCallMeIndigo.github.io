How it started

Meet Dimitris and Rebecca; after working hard in the hospitality industry for many years, they looked to share there

Where are we today?

Over the years the business has grown and so too have their family! They now look forward to welcoming you and your family to their new
and improved restaurant, “The Grill House”. We love The Grill House enjoy our fabulous Ribs, quality Steaks, food for all the family.
