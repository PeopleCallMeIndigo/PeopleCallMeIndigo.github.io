Meet our team

We are proven experts in the roadside safety and perimeter security industries with extensive experience in both the research and private sectors. We have developed numerous Intellectual Property (IP) to date and now with the ability to perform full-scale crash testing at an incredibly fast rate, our portfolio of products and services are expanding and revolutionizing safety.

Sana Moran is a highly skilled entrepreneur and researcher who has started three successful companies in the engineering and construction fields after working nearly a decade at Texas A&M Transportation Institute (TTI) as a Transportation Researcher alongside some of the most prominent engineers in the world while designing, testing, and analyzing roadside safety infrastructure and perimeter security systems.

After working as a researcher, Mr. Moran built a private roadside safety company from the ground up as their Vice President where he led the company's operations and invented or co-invented nearly ten roadside safety or perimeter security products. Mr. Moran holds BS and MS degrees in Civil Engineering from Texas A&M University.

Tyler Skinner is a very experienced researcher and construction manager that has a multitude of experience leading large-scale projects. Mr. Skinner spent several years at TTI alongside Sana as a researcher and helped design and analyze over twenty roadside safety devices/systems. He holds a BS degree in Civil Engineering from Lamar University.
