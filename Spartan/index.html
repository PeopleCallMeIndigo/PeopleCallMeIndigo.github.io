Spartan Premier, LLC business is a Texas Limited Liability company that focuses on the intellectual property development of roadside safety and perimeter security products/devices.

Company Mission Statement: To develop and implement roadside safety and perimeter security products that raise the standards for safety barriers and promote the well-being of society. We strive to implement the most innovative concepts to our designs that enable us to create cost-effective and resilient products with the highest integrity.

Testing Services

Provide clients with state-of-the-art testing capabilities. These capabilities include but are not limited to full-scale vehicular crash testing, surrogate vehicle physical crash testing, and small-scale pendulum testing. Our projects

Take a look at out portfolio

Project: Subdivision Development in Texas named Texas of Lakes of Champions Estates Subdivision.

Project Summary: Completed a thorough review of the subdivision plans and approved the plans leading to the construction of the subdivision. This included several meetings with the developer engineers, performing a complete hydrological analysis of the landscape to prevent flooding, and performing a final inspection of the subdivision site.

Project: Residential Construction

Project Summary: A project carried out in Saltillo Coahuila which was carried out successfully and with top quality. Thus we leave a more satisfied customer with our product which is guaranteed and made with the best materials to ensure optimal benefit and maximum safety.

Project: Parking Lot Construction

Project Summary: Poured 3 concrete parking lots for the Chambers County Courthouse and Jail, resurfaced the existing parking lot with a sealer, and repainted the existing parking lot. This project included removal and grading of existing soil, site stabilization, supervision of contractors who installed the concrete parking lot, inspection of the concrete parking lot, and layout of the locations.

Project: PennDOT - Implementation of new and innovative highway infrastructure.

Project Summary: Sana Moran was lead engineer for this project where he successfully led numerous phases to provide the state of Pennsylvania with fifty (50) highway safety barriers and other roadway infrastructure in use throughout the state of Pennsylvania. Mr. Skinner provided essential writing and analytical support that helped design numerous barriers and other traffic safety products.

Project: Texas Department of Transportation Highway System Assessment.

Project Summary: Mr. Moran and Mr. Skinner were part of the engineering team to develop improved highway infrastructure for the state of Texas. Mr. Moran successfully led numerous phases of this project to provide TxDOT with highway infrastructure that meets the latest safety standards. All barriers and other infrastructure designed by Mr. Moran successfully passed all required testing criteria.

Project: Road Milling and Resurfacing. Installation of sheet piles.

Project Summary: 400 meters of asphalt were milled and paved and 3 sheet piles were installed to reduce erosion around the bridge. This project included Geotech calculations to determine the required sheet pile strength, road preparation, traveler detouring, supervision of the contractors who installed the new surface, and inspection of the completed road and sheet piles.
